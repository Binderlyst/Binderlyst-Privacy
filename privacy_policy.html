Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25 May 2026

📄Overview

This page explains how Binderlyst handles your data. At this point in time, I don't collect personal information, run any analytics, show advertising, or share anything with third parties. Your collection stays on your device and the app only goes online to look up card data from a public database.

  • No account required.
  • No ads or tracking.
  • No third-party analytics.
  • Currently free.

📷Camera Access

Binderlyst asks for camera access so you can scan physical trading cards. When you tap the Scan button, the app reads text from the card image using on-device recognition. Nothing is uploaded or saved. The image exists only in memory for the moment it takes to identify the card, then it is gone.

  • Camera is only used when you actively tap Scan.
  • No photos are saved to your device or sent anywhere.
  • If you would prefer not to grant camera access, all other features still work.

📦Your Collection

Your collection data is currently stored locally on your device. There is no Binderlyst account and no server receiving your cards. I don't have access to your collection. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is removed with it, unless your device's Android auto-backup is enabled, in which case some data may be backed up to your Google account independently of this app.

I'm exploring additional storage options, such as cloud support, in the future. For now, I'd recommend exporting your collection regularly using the export options available within the app. CSV, Excel and PDF are all supported.

🌐Internet & Scryfall

The app connects to the internet to look up card information from Scryfall, a free and open trading card database. When you scan or search for a card, a query is sent containing only card identifiers such as a card name or set code. No personal information is ever part of those requests.

  • Network requests are made to Scryfall's servers for card data and card images.
  • Card images are loaded from Scryfall each session and are not stored permanently on your device.
  • No advertising, analytics, or crash reporting services are included in the app.

🚫What I Don't Collect

At this point in time, Binderlyst does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any login credentials.
  • Your location.
  • Your device ID or advertising ID.
  • Usage data, session time, or which features you use.
  • Crash reports or diagnostic logs.

🤖A Note on AI

I use AI to help develop and write code for Binderlyst. I understand this technology can raise concerns and strong opinions within parts of the community, and that's completely fair. Binderlyst would not exist without it. If this is something that prevents you from using the app, I completely understand and hope you find an alternative that works better for you.

💰Monetisation

Binderlyst is currently free and not monetised. As a new app, I'm exploring ways to support its development financially, but that's not my main priority right now. If anything changes, I'll keep users updated. My goal is to avoid intrusive ads, unnecessary annoyances, or paywalls that lock away core features.

📋Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, I'll revise the effective date at the top of the page. Any changes that meaningfully affect how data is handled will be communicated through an app update.

✉️Contact

If you have questions, feedback, concerns, or feature requests, you can reach me at binderlyst@hotmail.com. I'm currently studying while also working full-time, so responses may take a little time, but I'll do my best to get back to you as soon as I can.
