Privacy Policy

Effective date: 17 June 2026

🔍Overview

This page explains how Binderlyst handles your data. Binderlyst doesn't collect personal information, show advertising, or sell or share your data with third parties for their own purposes. Your collection stays on your device. The app uses limited, anonymous analytics and crash reporting to improve reliability (see below), and only goes online to look up card data from a public database, or (if you choose) to back up your data to your own Google Drive.

  • No account required to use the app.
  • No ads.
  • Anonymous usage analytics & crash reporting, used only to improve the app.
  • Optional Google Drive backup, fully under your control.
  • Currently free.

📷Camera Access

Binderlyst asks for camera access so you can scan physical trading cards. When you tap the Scan button, the app reads text from the card image using on-device recognition. Nothing is uploaded or saved. The image exists only in memory for the moment it takes to identify the card, then it is gone.

  • Camera is only used when you actively tap Scan.
  • No photos are saved to your device or sent anywhere.
  • If you would prefer not to grant camera access, all other features still work.

📦Your Collection

By default, your collection data is stored locally on your device. Binderlyst has no built-in account system and no server that receives your cards, so your collection isn't accessible to the developer. If you uninstall the app, locally stored data is removed with it, unless your device's Android auto-backup is enabled, in which case some data may be backed up to your Google account independently of this app.

You can also back up your data to your own Google Drive (see Google Drive Backup below), and you can export your collection at any time using the in-app export options. CSV, Excel and PDF are all supported.

☁️Google Drive Backup

Binderlyst offers an optional backup feature that saves a copy of your data to your own Google Drive, so you can restore it if you lose or change your device. This only happens when you choose to sign in with Google and tap Back up (or enable automatic backup). It is off until you turn it on.

  • What's backed up: your collection, decks, folders, saved reports, dashboard layout, app settings, and any dashboard attachments, saved as a single backup file in your Drive.
  • Binderlyst uses the limited drive.file permission. This means the app can only see and manage the backup file it creates. It cannot see, read, or touch any of your other Google Drive files.
  • The backup is stored in your Google Drive. There is no Binderlyst server, and the developer has no access to your backup or your Google account.
  • Your Google account email may be shown inside the app to indicate that you are signed in. This is stored only on your device and is never sent to the developer.
  • You stay in control: you can sign out, turn off backup, or delete the backup file from your Google Drive at any time.

Once data is in your Google Drive, Google's handling of it is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

🌐Internet & Scryfall

The app connects to the internet to look up card information from Scryfall, a free and open trading card database. When you scan or search for a card, a query is sent containing only card identifiers such as a card name or set code. No personal information is ever part of those requests.

  • Network requests are made to Scryfall's servers for card data and card images.
  • Card images are loaded from Scryfall each session and are not stored permanently on your device.
  • No advertising is shown in the app.

📊Analytics & Crash Reporting

To understand how the app is used and to find and fix bugs, Binderlyst uses Google's Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics. This collects limited, aggregated technical information. It never includes your collection, the cards you save, or anything that personally identifies you.

  • Usage events such as screen views and in-app actions (for example: scanning a card, creating a deck, or running a backup).
  • Device and app information: device model, operating-system version, app version, and language.
  • An approximate (country/region-level) location derived from your IP address, never precise or GPS location.
  • Crash diagnostics: the error and a technical stack trace when the app crashes, so it can be fixed.

This data is processed by Google as described in the Firebase Privacy and Security documentation. It is not used to identify you and is never sold or shared for advertising. The app's advertising identifier is not collected.

🚫What Binderlyst Doesn't Collect

Binderlyst does not collect, store on any server, or send to the developer any of the following:

  • Your name, password, or any login credentials. When you sign in for backup, that is handled securely by Google, and Binderlyst never sees your password.
  • Your precise or GPS location.
  • Your advertising ID (its collection is disabled).
  • The contents of your collection, decks, or the card data you save. These stay on your device (and, if you choose, in your own Google Drive).

🤖A Note on AI

Binderlyst is built with the help of AI tools for writing and developing its code. This technology can raise concerns and strong opinions within parts of the community, and that's completely fair. Binderlyst would not exist without it. If this is something that prevents you from using the app, that's understandable, and we hope you find an alternative that works better for you.

💰Monetisation

Binderlyst is currently free and not monetised. As a new app, ways to support its development financially may be explored in future, but that isn't the priority right now. If anything changes, users will be kept updated. The goal is to avoid intrusive ads, unnecessary annoyances, or paywalls that lock away core features.

📝Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the effective date at the top of the page will be revised. Any changes that meaningfully affect how data is handled will be communicated through an app update.

✉️Contact

If you have questions, feedback, concerns, or feature requests, you can get in touch at binderlyst@hotmail.com. Responses may take a little time, but you'll get a reply as soon as possible.
